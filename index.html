Kay Pee Eff Tooltech, formally known as Krishna Press Form, is a customer-driven company located in Hosur, the industrial town of Tamil Nadu. We are specialized in Manufacturing Sheet metal pressed components catering to the requirements of OEMs of Automobile industries. The company has been evolving with technological brilliance since it came into existence in 2007, keeping pace with emerging technological trends. The constant up-gradation of technology, processes, and the die-hard conviction for quality, brought laurels, awards, rewards, recognition, certification, and above all, the trust and confidence of our most valued customers.
